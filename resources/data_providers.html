  • General sources
    • Data.gov, a general U.S. Government open data catalog

    • USGS National map URLs for services
    • NC OneMap
    • NC Spatial Data Download: QL2 2014 LiDAR data

    • Google Earth Engine
  • Topography
    • USGS NED, NLCD from National Map Viewer (NED – National Elevation Data, NLCD – National Land Cover Data)
    • Elevation derivatives for National Applications (EDNA)
    • USGS Center for LIDAR Information (CLICK)
    • Digital Coast (includes former LDART)
    • SRTM Shuttle Radar Topography Mission
    • SRTM V4 Data: Global DEM
    • NC digital flood maps and lidar elevation data
    • OpenTopography, lidar data (includes e.g. Haiti earthquake)
    • Lidar online: sharing lidar data from all over the world
  • Natural and environmental conditions
    • USGS Global Land cover data portal
    • Open Land service (China, GEO, ISPRS) Global Land cover data portal
    • LANDEX: map locations with similar land cover pattern
    • LANDSAT imagery: global locator
    • USGS Emergency Response (HDDS), hazards data
    • State Climatology Office
    • North Carolina groundwater data
    • Real-time water data (streams and lakes)
    • EPA surf your watershed
    • EPA Air quality
    • USDA natural resources data
    • MODIS (Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer)
  • Socio-economic conditions, energy, transportation
    • OpenStreetMap
    • City Data
    • Census 2010 data
    • Renewable energy calculator
    • Photovoltaic WebGIS
    • iMAPS: Wake county GIS
